Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Flow Lines is designed as a fully offline single-player puzzle game. We do not require account registration, and we do not intentionally collect personal information from users through the app.

Information We Collect

Flow Lines does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers. Gameplay progress, unlocked levels, music settings, and related preferences are stored locally on your device only.

How Data Is Used

Local device data is used only to preserve your game progress and settings, such as level completion, star ratings, and audio preferences.

Network Access

Flow Lines does not require an internet connection for gameplay. The app is intended to work offline.

Third-Party Services

Flow Lines does not include third-party analytics, third-party advertising SDKs, or account-based tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because Flow Lines does not collect personal information, it is designed to minimize privacy risk for users of all ages. Parents or guardians may still review device-level permissions and settings on their own hardware.

Data Retention

All saved data remains on your device until you remove the app or clear its local data through your device settings.

Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time and remove locally stored data by uninstalling Flow Lines from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's privacy practices change in a future release, this policy will be updated accordingly before or at the time those changes take effect.
